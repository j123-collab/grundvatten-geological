Development and action plan for securing groundwater resources. And for the establishment of groundwater sources 2025–2027.

My name is Lars Falk. My colleagues and I have, over almost three decades (founded 1992), developed a programme and business concept for locating and evaluating suitable land areas to establish wells, and to produce and secure drinking water from groundwater sources in Scandinavia.

Today, our water resources are a highly topical issue, and public awareness of the importance of having access to clean water cannot be taken for granted.

To supply 50,000 residents/personnel with approximately 3 litres of drinking water per day requires access to 150,000 litres per day — or 54,750,000 litres per year — from a secured drinking water well.

Over the years, we have continuously updated our development and action plan for groundwater sources for the target groups we have focused on since the 1990s.

The global situation is entirely different today, and our chosen target groups are the Armed Forces, Police and Rescue Services, Municipalities, and Institutions in each respective country.

Säki Today, 2025–2027, our entire development and action plan for groundwater is classified for security and confidentiality. We present no details on our website — this is initially communicated verbally upon request, during a physical meeting.
